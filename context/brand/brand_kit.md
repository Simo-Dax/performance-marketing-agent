# Brand Strategy & UX/UI Guidelines: Indie Productivity

## 1. Brand Identity
Indie Productivity is a platform focused on sharing practical resources for professionals and companies. The brand personality is:
*   **Energetic:** Driven by the vibrant yellow.
*   **Approachable:** Personal brand feel (Simone Dassereto).
*   **Clean & Efficient:** Minimalist layout to reflect productivity.

## 2. Target Audience
*   Solopreneurs and indie hackers.
*   Marketing and management professionals.
*   Small to medium business owners looking for growth strategies.

## 3. Design Principles
*   **High Clarity:** Content is king. Use ample whitespace.
*   **Bold Typography:** Strong headlines to establish hierarchy.
*   **Action-Oriented:** CTAs (like the newsletter signup) should be the most prominent element.
*   **Consistency:** Use a fixed grid and consistent spacing (8px system).

## 4. Visual Elements
*   **Imagery:** Professional but friendly portraits.
*   **Icons:** Minimalist line art.
*   **Logos:** Monochromatic (black/white) to not compete with the brand yellow.

## 5. UI Components Checklist
*   Sticky Navigation Bar.
*   Hero section with clear value proposition.
*   Social proof/Logo cloud (as seen in the screenshot).
*   Newsletter subscription blocks.
*   Blog card layouts with clear categories.
