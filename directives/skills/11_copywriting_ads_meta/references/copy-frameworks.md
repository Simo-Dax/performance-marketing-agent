# Copy Frameworks

Use these frameworks to structure the body copy of Meta Ads. Apply the framework strictly when the user requests a specific one. When no framework is specified, choose the most appropriate one for the funnel stage and angle.

---

## PAS (Problem - Agitation - Solution)

Best for: Awareness and Consideration stages. Pain-point-driven ads.

**Structure:**
1. **Problem**: State the problem the target audience faces. Be specific and relatable.
2. **Agitation**: Amplify the emotional weight of the problem. What happens if they don't solve it? How does it feel?
3. **Solution**: Introduce the product/service as the resolution. Keep it benefit-focused.

**Example pattern (Italian):**
- P: "[Problema specifico del target]"
- A: "[Conseguenza emotiva o pratica del problema]"
- S: "[Come il prodotto risolve il problema + CTA]"

---

## AIDA (Attention - Interest - Desire - Action)

Best for: Full funnel, especially Awareness and Conversion. Versatile.

**Structure:**
1. **Attention**: Open with a bold, unexpected, or curiosity-driven statement.
2. **Interest**: Provide a relevant fact, insight, or scenario that keeps them reading.
3. **Desire**: Connect the product's benefits to what the reader wants. Make them feel the outcome.
4. **Action**: Clear, specific call to action.

**Example pattern (Italian):**
- A: "[Frase d'impatto o domanda provocatoria]"
- I: "[Fatto rilevante o scenario]"
- D: "[Beneficio concreto legato al desiderio del target]"
- A: "[CTA diretta]"

---

## BAB (Before - After - Bridge)

Best for: Consideration and Conversion. Transformation-focused ads.

**Structure:**
1. **Before**: Describe where the user is now. The current frustrating or unsatisfying situation.
2. **After**: Paint the picture of life after the problem is solved. Be vivid and specific.
3. **Bridge**: Introduce the product as the bridge between Before and After.

**Example pattern (Italian):**
- Before: "[Situazione attuale frustrante]"
- After: "[Visione del risultato desiderato]"
- Bridge: "[Il prodotto come collegamento tra i due stati + CTA]"

---

## 4Ps (Picture - Promise - Prove - Push)

Best for: Conversion and Remarketing. Persuasion-heavy ads.

**Structure:**
1. **Picture**: Create a vivid scene the prospect can see themselves in. Either a current pain or an aspirational future.
2. **Promise**: State clearly how your solution ends that pain or delivers that future.
3. **Prove**: Back up the promise. Use social proof, guarantees, consequences of failure, or demonstrations.
4. **Push**: Nudge toward action. Create a reason to act now.

**Example pattern (Italian):**
- Picture: "[Scena vivida: dolore attuale o futuro desiderato]"
- Promise: "[Come il prodotto garantisce il risultato]"
- Prove: "[Prova: testimonianza, garanzia, dato concreto]"
- Push: "[Spinta all'azione + CTA]"

---

## Framework Selection Guide

| Funnel Stage | Recommended Frameworks |
|---|---|
| Awareness | PAS, AIDA |
| Consideration | BAB, AIDA, PAS |
| Conversion | 4Ps, BAB, AIDA |
| Remarketing | 4Ps, PAS |
